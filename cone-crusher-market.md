<p><p>A cone crusher is a type of crushing equipment that uses a conical-shaped mantle and a bowl to crush materials, primarily in the aggregate and mining industries. Cone crushers are highly effective for secondary, tertiary, and quaternary crushing applications.</p><p>The Cone Crusher Market is witnessing steady growth, driven by increasing demand for construction and mining activities globally.
