<p><strong>The Truck Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diagonal Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck tire market primarily consists of two types: diagonal tires and radial tires. Diagonal tires, characterized by alternating tire plies, offer strength and durability for off-road and tough terrains. They provide a stiffer sidewall, making them suitable for heavy loads. In contrast, radial tires feature plies that run perpendicular to the tire's direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, improved traction, and enhanced ride comfort. Radial tires are preferred for long-haul trucking due to their superior performance on highways.</p></p>
