<p><ul><li>Dynamic Image Analysis</li><li>Static Image Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Powder Flowability Testers market encompasses two primary types: Dynamic Image Analysis and Static Image Analysis. Dynamic Image Analysis measures the flow characteristics of powders under varying conditions, capturing real-time behavior as powders move, which helps in understanding their flowability during processing. In contrast, Static Image Analysis evaluates powder flowability in a stationary state, focusing on particle size, shape, and distribution without movement, providing insights into potential issues during storage and handling. Both methods are crucial for optimizing powder performance in various applications.</p></p>
