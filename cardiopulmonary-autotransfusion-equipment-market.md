<p><p>Cardiopulmonary autotransfusion equipment primarily comprises two market types: unwashed autotransfusion systems (ATS) and washed ATS. Unwashed ATS collects and reinfuses blood without additional processing, retaining some contaminants, while washed ATS undergoes a cleansing process to remove impurities and leukocytes, enhancing safety and reducing complications. The washed ATS market is growing as it offers a higher quality of reinfused blood, making it preferable for surgical procedures, particularly in cardiothoracic surgeries, where patient safety is paramount.</p></p>
