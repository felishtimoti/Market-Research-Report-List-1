<p><p>The sterile tissue adhesive market comprises various types catering to different medical needs. Protein-based adhesives harness natural proteins for tissue bonding. Collagen-based tissue adhesives utilize collagen to promote healing and integration with biological tissues. Thrombin-based adhesives leverage the enzyme thrombin for effective hemostasis and tissue adhesion. Fibrin adhesives mimic the final stage of blood clotting, facilitating secure tissue connection. Other adhesives in this market may include synthetic options or innovative alternatives designed for specific surgical applications, enhancing versatility in medical procedures.</p></p>
