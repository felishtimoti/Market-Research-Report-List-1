<p><strong>The Range Finder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The range finder market is primarily segmented into laser and ultrasonic types. Laser range finders utilize laser beams to measure distances with high precision, making them ideal for applications in surveying, construction, and outdoor activities. In contrast, ultrasonic range finders use sound waves to determine distance, typically suited for short-range applications like industrial automation and robotics. Both technologies cater to different needs, with laser options offering accuracy over long distances, while ultrasonic devices excel in simpler, shorter-range measurements.</p></p>
