<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Long-wave Infrared Camera</li><li>Uncooled Middle-wave Infrared Camera</li><li>Uncooled Short-wave Infrared Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled infrared camera market is categorized into three main types based on the infrared spectrum. Uncooled long-wave infrared (LWIR) cameras operate in the 8-14 micrometer range and are ideal for thermal imaging in various applications. Uncooled middle-wave infrared (MWIR) cameras, covering 3-5 micrometers, are used primarily for industrial and military purposes. Lastly, uncooled short-wave infrared (SWIR) cameras, working within the 1-3 micrometer range, are suitable for applications like surveillance and quality control in manufacturing.</p></p>
