<p><strong>The Luffing Stackers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slewing</li><li>Non Slewing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luffing stackers are categorized into two main market types: slewing and non-slewing. Slewing luffing stackers feature a rotating upper section, allowing for flexible maneuvering and precise positioning of materials. They are often used in tight spaces where lateral movement is essential. In contrast, non-slewing luffing stackers operate without rotation, making them more stable and suitable for long repetitive stacking tasks. Both types serve crucial roles in material handling, particularly in ports and cargo facilities, enhancing efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
