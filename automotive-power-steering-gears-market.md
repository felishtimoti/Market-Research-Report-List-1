<p><p>The automotive power steering gears market comprises three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electric Power Steering (EPS), and Other systems. HPS uses hydraulic fluid to assist steering, providing a traditional, responsive feel but can be less efficient. EPS employs electric motors for assistance, offering better fuel efficiency and precise control while reducing weight and maintenance. Other systems may include hybrids or advanced technologies combining features of both HPS and EPS, aiming for enhanced performance and adaptability in various driving conditions.</p></p>
