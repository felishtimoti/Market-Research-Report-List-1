<p><p>The bare-metal vascular stents market is divided into three primary types: coronary stents, peripheral stents, and others. Coronary stents are designed for use in the coronary arteries to treat blockages and improve blood flow to the heart. Peripheral stents are used in other blood vessels outside the heart, such as those in the legs or arms, to address vascular issues. The 'others' category may include specialized stents for various unique vascular applications. Each type plays a crucial role in cardiovascular health management.</p></p>
