<p><ul><li>Electroanatomical Mapping</li><li>Basket Catheter Mapping</li><li>Traditional Endocardial Catheter Mapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact Heart Mapping encompasses various market types in cardiac diagnostics. Electroanatomical Mapping utilizes advanced imaging to create detailed 3D maps of the heart's electrical activity, aiding precise interventions. Basket Catheter Mapping involves specialized catheters that capture data from multiple sites simultaneously, enhancing arrhythmia diagnosis. Traditional Endocardial Catheter Mapping, the most established method, involves inserting catheters directly into the heart chambers to assess electrical signals but is limited by a more localized perspective. Each method offers unique advantages for improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
