<p><p>The e-textile market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Passive Electronic Textiles**: These fabrics do not require an external power source and function by integrating conductive materials that enable basic functionalities like sensing.  </p><p>2. **Active Electronic Textiles**: These textiles incorporate energy sources, such as batteries or solar cells, enabling them to power devices or features like heating elements and advanced sensors.  </p><p>3. **Ultra-Electronic Textiles**: These represent the most advanced category, integrating sophisticated components like microprocessors and wireless communication systems for seamless data exchange and enhanced interactivity.</p></p>
