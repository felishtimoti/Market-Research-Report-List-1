Disc chippers utilize a rotating disc to slice wood into uniform chips, ideal for producing high-quality mulch. Screw chippers employ a helical screw mechanism for efficient processing of softer materials. Drum chippers feature a drum with sharp blades, excelling in handling larger logs and bulky waste.
