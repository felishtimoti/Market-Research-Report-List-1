<p><ul><li>Front</li><li>Rear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pet bike carrier market primarily comprises two types: front and rear carriers. Front carriers attach to the handlebars, allowing owners to keep their pets close while cycling, providing an interactive experience. They are ideal for smaller pets and offer better visibility for the rider. Rear carriers, positioned on the back of the bike, typically provide more space and stability for larger pets. They are designed for longer rides, ensuring comfort and safety while balancing the bike's weight distribution.</p></p>
