<p><ul><li>Surface Contact Sensors</li><li>Recessed Contact Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Door and Window Sensors market features two primary types: Surface Contact Sensors and Recessed Contact Sensors. Surface Contact Sensors are mounted on the surface of doors and windows, making them easy to install and visible. In contrast, Recessed Contact Sensors are installed within the door or window frame, providing a discreet solution that’s less noticeable. Both types serve to detect unauthorized entry, enhancing security systems by alerting homeowners and businesses when doors or windows are opened unexpectedly.</p></p>
