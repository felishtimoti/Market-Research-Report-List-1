<p><p>The exterior mirror market is categorized into two main types: manual adjustment and automatic adjustment mirrors. Manual adjustment mirrors require the driver to physically reposition the mirror by hand to achieve the desired angle, often found in more basic vehicle models. In contrast, automatic adjustment mirrors utilize electronic controls, allowing drivers to adjust the mirror settings using buttons or sensors, enhancing convenience and visibility. The choice between these types often depends on vehicle features, cost, and consumer preferences for ease of use.</p></p>
