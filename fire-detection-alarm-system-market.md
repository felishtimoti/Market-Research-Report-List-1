<p><strong>The Fire Detection Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Fire Alarm Systems</li><li>Addressable Fire Alarm Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire detection alarm system market primarily consists of two types: Conventional Fire Alarm Systems and Addressable Fire Alarm Systems. Conventional systems operate on a zone-based approach, triggering alarms for specific areas without pinpointed identification of individual devices, making them cost-effective for smaller installations. Conversely, Addressable Fire Alarm Systems offer advanced functionality by assigning a unique identifier to each device, allowing precise location tracking and faster response times, making them suitable for larger, more complex facilities where rapid detection is critical.</p></p>
