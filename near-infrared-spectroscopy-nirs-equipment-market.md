<p><p>The Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) equipment market comprises several types, primarily including FT-NIR Spectroscopy and Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy. FT-NIR utilizes Fourier-transform technology for rapid data acquisition and analysis, ideal for complex samples. Dispersive NIR employs diffraction for wavelength separation, suitable for qualitative and quantitative analysis but often slower than FT-NIR. Additionally, Other types like Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTF) and filter-based systems offer specific applications, providing flexibility and adaptability across various industries, including pharmaceuticals and agriculture.</p></p>
