<p><strong>The AC Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-Loop Current Sensors</li><li>Open Loop Current Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC current sensors market is primarily divided into two main types: closed-loop and open-loop current sensors. Closed-loop current sensors, which utilize feedback mechanisms, offer high accuracy and better linearity, making them suitable for precise applications. In contrast, open-loop current sensors operate based on the magnetic field generated by the current, providing simpler designs and lower costs, but with less accuracy. Each type serves specific applications, from energy monitoring to industrial automation, reflecting the diverse needs of users in the market.</p></p>
