Air sterilization involves the use of UV light to eliminate airborne pathogens as air circulates through the system, improving indoor air quality. Coil sterilization targets the cooling coils within HVAC units, using UV light to reduce microbial growth and prevent mold, thereby enhancing system efficiency and longevity. Both methods contribute to healthier indoor environments and optimized HVAC performance.</p></p>
