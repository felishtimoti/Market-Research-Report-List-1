<p><strong>The Medical Lithotripter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intracorporeal Lithotripter</li><li>Extracorporeal Lithotripter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical lithotripter market is primarily categorized into two types: intracorporeal and extracorporeal lithotripters. Intracorporeal lithotripters are used within the body to target and break down stones using devices like laser or ultrasound directly at the site. In contrast, extracorporeal lithotripters operate outside the body, employing shock waves to fragment stones non-invasively. Both types are essential for treating conditions like urinary stones, offering varying approaches based on patient needs and the complexity of the case.</p></p>
