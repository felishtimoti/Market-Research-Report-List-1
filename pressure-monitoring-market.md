<p><p>The pressure monitoring market encompasses various types of devices designed to measure different body pressures for medical diagnostics. BP monitors assess blood pressure to manage cardiovascular health. Cardiac pressure monitors gauge pressures in the heart to detect abnormalities. Pulmonary pressure monitors evaluate lung pressures for respiratory conditions. Intraocular pressure monitors are crucial for diagnosing glaucoma and other eye disorders. Intracranial pressure monitors track brain pressure, vital for neurological health. Additional devices include specialized monitors for other specific medical applications.</p></p>
