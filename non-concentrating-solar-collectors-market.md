<p><p>Non-concentrating solar collectors are devices designed to capture solar energy without the use of mirrors or lenses to concentrate sunlight. Aluminum plates are lightweight and cost-effective, ideal for residential applications. Steel absorber plates, while less efficient, offer durability and lower production costs, making them suitable for larger installations. Each type serves different market segments based on efficiency, cost, and application needs.</p></p>
