<p><p>The commercial rice cooker market comprises three main types: IH (Induction Heating) rice cookers, electric rice cookers, and gas rice cookers. IH rice cookers use induction technology for precise temperature control, ensuring even cooking and consistent texture. Electric rice cookers rely on electric heating elements, offering convenience and ease of use for large-scale rice preparation. Gas rice cookers utilize gas flames for cooking, providing quick heating and a traditional approach, often favored in outdoor or large kitchen settings. Each type serves specific culinary needs.</p></p>
