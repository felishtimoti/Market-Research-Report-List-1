<p><p>The MRI Safe Pacemaker market includes various types designed for patients requiring cardiac rhythm management while ensuring compatibility with MRI scans. Leadless Pacemakers are compact devices implanted directly in the heart, eliminating leads. Single-chamber Pacemakers regulate one heart chamber, while Dual-chamber Pacemakers coordinate both atria and ventricles. Biventricular Pacemakers are advanced devices used in cardiac resynchronization therapy, improving heart function by synchronizing contractions. These options enhance patient safety and comfort during MRI procedures while addressing diverse cardiac needs.</p></p>
