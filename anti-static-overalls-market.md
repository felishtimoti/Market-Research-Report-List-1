<p><p>The anti-static overalls market can be categorized into two primary types: split type and Siamese type. Split type overalls feature a design that allows for separation between the upper and lower sections, offering flexibility and ease of movement, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, Siamese type overalls are constructed as a single piece, providing complete coverage and enhanced protection against static discharge. Both types are crucial in environments where electrostatic discharge poses a risk to sensitive electronic components.</p></p>
