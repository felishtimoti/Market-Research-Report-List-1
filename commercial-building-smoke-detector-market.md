<p><strong>The Commercial Building Smoke Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Type</li><li>Ionization Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial building smoke detector market comprises three primary types: photoelectric, ionization, and others. Photoelectric detectors use light beams to sense smoke particles, making them effective for slow-smoldering fires. Ionization detectors, on the other hand, rely on radioactive materials to detect fast-flaming fires through changes in ion flow. "Others" encompass hybrid detectors and specialized systems designed for unique applications or environmental conditions. Each type serves distinct fire safety requirements, influencing market demand and preferences in various commercial settings.</p></p>
