<p><p>The super abrasive market comprises three primary types: diamond, cubic boron nitride (CBN), and other abrasives. Diamond is known for its exceptional hardness and is primarily used for cutting, grinding, and polishing hard materials. CBN, slightly less hard than diamond but more thermally stable, is ideal for machining hardened steel and high-speed applications. Other abrasives include materials like synthetic or natural garnet, which serve niche applications. Each type plays a crucial role in various industries, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
