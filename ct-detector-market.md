<p><ul><li>Single Detector</li><li>Multi-Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT detector market is divided into two main types: single detector and multi-detector systems. Single detector systems utilize a single x-ray beam and a single detector, offering simplicity and lower cost, suitable for basic imaging needs. In contrast, multi-detector systems feature multiple rows of detectors, enabling faster scanning, improved image resolution, and enhanced volumetric imaging capabilities. These systems are increasingly favored in clinical settings for their efficiency and advanced diagnostic capabilities, particularly in complex examinations.</p></p>
