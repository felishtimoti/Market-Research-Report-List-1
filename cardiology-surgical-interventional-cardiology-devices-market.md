<p><p>The cardiology surgical and interventional devices market comprises various types of heart surgery techniques. Open-heart surgery involves traditional methods requiring large chest incisions to repair heart conditions. Off-pump heart surgery is performed without cardiopulmonary bypass, allowing surgeons to operate on a beating heart, which can reduce recovery time. Minimally invasive heart surgery uses smaller incisions, often with robotic assistance, leading to quicker recovery and less postoperative pain. Each technique offers unique benefits, catering to different patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
