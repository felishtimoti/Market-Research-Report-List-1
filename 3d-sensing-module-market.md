<p><p>The 3D sensing module market primarily includes two key technologies: Time-of-Flight (ToF) and Structured Light. ToF technology measures the time it takes for light pulses to travel to an object and back, enabling accurate depth perception and real-time 3D mapping. Structured Light uses projected patterns to identify depth by analyzing distortions caused when the patterns hit surfaces. Both technologies find applications in various sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare, enhancing user experiences through advanced sensing capabilities.</p></p>
