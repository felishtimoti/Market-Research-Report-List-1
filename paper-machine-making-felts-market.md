<p><ul><li>Wet Felt</li><li>Upper Felt</li><li>Dry Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper machine making felts market consists of three primary types: Wet Felt, Upper Felt, and Dry Felt. Wet Felt is utilized for dewatering paper in the wet end, enhancing drainage and sheet formation. Upper Felt, positioned above the paper sheet, aids in maintaining even pressure while absorbing moisture. Dry Felt operates in the dryer section, promoting heat transfer and moisture removal. Each type plays a critical role in optimizing the papermaking process, ensuring efficiency and quality in final products.</p></p>
