<p><p>The refractive surgical devices market comprises various technologies that enhance vision correction. Femtosecond lasers utilize ultra-short pulses to create precise corneal incisions, improving surgical outcomes in procedures like LASIK. Excimer lasers employ ultraviolet light to reshape the cornea, effectively treating refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Other devices include implantable lenses and wavefront-guided systems that offer alternatives and enhancements to traditional laser methods, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in vision correction.</p></p>
