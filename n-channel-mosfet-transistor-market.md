<p><ul><li>Enhancement Type</li><li>Depletion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The N-channel MOSFET transistor market encompasses two primary types: enhancement mode and depletion mode. Enhancement-type MOSFETs require a positive gate voltage to conduct, making them widely used in digital circuits and power applications due to their efficiency and high speed. In contrast, depletion-type MOSFETs are normally on and require a negative voltage to turn off, catering to specific analog applications. Together, these types address diverse needs in power management, signal processing, and switching applications across various industries.</p></p>
