<p><p>The fiberglass market comprises various types, including roving glass fibers, chopped glass fibers, yarn glass fibers, and others. Roving glass fibers are continuous strands used for reinforcement in composite materials. Chopped glass fibers are short strands ideal for producing thermoplastics and thermosets, enhancing strength and durability. Yarn glass fibers consist of twisted strands used in textiles and composite applications. Other types may include specialty fibers for unique applications, catering to diverse industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
