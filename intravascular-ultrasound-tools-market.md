<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) tools market is categorized into three main types: IVUS consoles, IVUS catheters, and accessories. IVUS consoles are specialized devices that generate and process ultrasound images from within blood vessels, crucial for diagnostic imaging. IVUS catheters are flexible tubes equipped with ultrasound transducers, enabling detailed visualization of vascular structures during procedures. Accessories include essential items like guidewires and sheaths that support catheter insertion and function, enhancing the effectiveness and safety of IVUS procedures in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
