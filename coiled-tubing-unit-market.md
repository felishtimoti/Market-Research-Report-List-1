<p><p>A Coiled Tubing Unit (CTU) is a specialized equipment used in the oil and gas industry for various operations, including drilling, well intervention, and workover activities. The unit consists of a continuous length of tubing that is wound on a reel, allowing for efficient transportation and deployment into the wellbore. This technology offers significant advantages, such as reduced operational downtime, improved safety, and the ability to perform multiple operations without removing the tubing from the well.</p><p>The Coiled Tubing Unit Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for enhanced oil recovery and the rise in unconventional oil and gas exploration activities.
