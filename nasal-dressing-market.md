<p><strong>The Nasal Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bioresorbable</li><li>Not Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nasal dressing market comprises two primary types: bioresorbable and not absorbable. Bioresorbable dressings are designed to gradually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for removal and reducing patient discomfort. They often promote healing while minimizing complications. In contrast, not absorbable dressings are intended for temporary use and require physical removal after a specific period. These are typically used in situations where immediate absorption is not necessary but the management of nasal bleeding or post-operative care is essential.</p></p>
