<p><p>The commercial natural gas water heaters market is primarily divided into two types: tankless and tank water heaters. Tankless natural gas water heaters provide on-demand hot water by heating it directly as it flows through the unit, which enhances energy efficiency and saves space. In contrast, tank natural gas water heaters store a significant volume of hot water in an insulated tank, supplying a steady flow, making them ideal for high-demand scenarios. Each type caters to different needs based on capacity and usage patterns.</p></p>
