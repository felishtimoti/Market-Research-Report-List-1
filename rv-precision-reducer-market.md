<p><p>The RV Precision Reducer market encompasses various types of gear systems designed for motion control and torque amplification. Spur gears are characterized by straight teeth and are effective for transmitting power between parallel shafts, ensuring high efficiency and reliability. Differential gears, on the other hand, allow for variations in rotational speed between connected shafts, making them ideal for applications requiring smooth operation and improved maneuverability. Both types are essential in robotics, automation, and industrial machinery, enhancing performance and precision.</p></p>
