<p><strong>The Containerized Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generator</li><li>PSA Nitrogen Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The containerized nitrogen generator market primarily consists of two types: membrane nitrogen generators and PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) nitrogen generators. Membrane nitrogen generators use selective permeability to separate nitrogen from oxygen, offering a compact and energy-efficient solution for on-site nitrogen production. In contrast, PSA nitrogen generators utilize adsorption principles to separate nitrogen from compressed air, providing high purity nitrogen and flexibility in varying production needs. Both types cater to diverse industries requiring readily available nitrogen solutions.</p></p>
