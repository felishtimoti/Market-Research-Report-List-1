<p><p>The manual transfer switch (MTS) market is categorized into panel-mounted and din rail-mounted types. Panel-mounted switches are typically installed in electrical control panels and are favored for their robust design and ease of use in residential and commercial settings. Din rail-mounted switches, on the other hand, are designed for efficient installation on standard din rails, making them ideal for compact electrical cabinets and industrial applications. Both types facilitate seamless switching between power sources during outages, enhancing reliability and safety.</p></p>
